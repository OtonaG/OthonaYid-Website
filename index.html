About Us

Otona Agricultural Products Wholesale Trade One Member Plc Import and Export is a dynamic company engaged in both exporting and importing business lines. Since our establishment, we have built a strong foundation with well-established management and technical staff. We are currently in a business expansion process, aiming to enhance our export capabilities while continuing our robust import operations.

Our company operates with a paid-up capital of 40,000,000.00 Birr. We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

We have a strong banking relationship, particularly with Nib Bank's Lideta branch, which has been instrumental in our import activities and foreign exchange transactions. We have successfully generated over 182,400 USD through this branch.

Our annual average turnover is approximately 76.0 million Birr, reflecting the high demand for our products and services and our operational efficiency.
